About jomPOMO

jomPOMO is a website that on its basis, is a pomodoro timer website. The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that helps improve productivity and focus by breaking work into intervals, typically 25 minutes long, called "Pomodoros," followed by a short 5-minute break. After completing four Pomodoros, you take a longer break of 15 to 30 minutes. This technique helps maintain concentration and prevents burnout by providing regular opportunities to rest and recharge.
