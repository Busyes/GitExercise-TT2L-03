Jom study with jomPOMO

jomPOMO is a website created to allow you to focus on a task by following the pomodoro rule. The pomodoro rule is a time management method based on 25-minute stretches of focused work broken by five-minute break. This technique works especially well for procrastinators and perfectionists alike.
